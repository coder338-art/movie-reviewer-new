Job Description

WatchPaid is looking for passionate film fans to join our remote content evaluation team. As a Movie Reviewer, you will watch newly released and upcoming films across all genres and submit structured written reviews, star ratings, and audience appeal assessments. Your feedback directly shapes our editorial recommendations and helps audiences discover content they'll love. No prior professional experience is required - just a genuine passion for film and the ability to write clearly.

What You Will Do

  • Watch assigned films, documentaries, and short features on a flexible schedule
  • Submit detailed written reviews covering plot, pacing, cinematography, and audience fit
  • Assign star ratings and genre tags to each title
  • Complete a minimum of 4 reviews per week at your own pace
  • Provide early-access feedback on unreleased titles when available

Requirements

  • Access to a streaming device or browser (subscriptions provided if needed)
  • Strong written communication skills in English
  • Reliable internet connection
  • Genuine interest in film across a variety of genres
  • No experience required - full onboarding and style guide provided

Benefits

  • $20 – $40 per hour based on review quality and volume
  • Flexible schedule - review on your own time
  • 100% remote - work from anywhere
  • Free streaming subscriptions included
  • Early access to unreleased and festival films
