About WatchPaid

Connecting people with flexible remote work since 2021

Who we are

WatchPaid is a remote opportunity platform dedicated to connecting film and television lovers with paid reviewing work. We believe that if you spend hours watching content anyway, you should be getting paid for your opinion.

We work with media companies, streaming platforms, and content agencies to bring you legitimate, flexible, work-from-home reviewing opportunities that require no prior experience.

What we offer

Our current featured opportunity is a Remote Movie Reviewer role paying $20 - $40 per hour. You watch assigned films and shows, submit written reviews and ratings, and get paid on a flexible schedule from anywhere in the world.

No commute. No experience required. No fixed hours. Just a genuine love of film and the ability to write clearly.
